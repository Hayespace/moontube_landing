Your Security

The signup process for Moontube is designed to ensure both security and authenticity for YouTubers. To register:

  1. Simply provide a link to your YouTube channel.
  2. You will receive a one-time password (OTP) sent to your email for verification.
  3. After verification, create an unlisted video that includes a unique code generated by our platform.
  4. Upload the video and share the link with us for manual verification.

Our team manually verifies each YouTuber to ensure authenticity. Importantly, you will never need to share your YouTube credentials—these remain secure and private.

Should any unauthorised party gain access to your Moontube account, our system only allows video submissions from your verified YouTube account, preventing the use of unauthorised links. This provides an additional layer of protection to safeguard your content and account.
